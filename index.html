About DollyWood

DollyWood is a theme park located in Pigeon Forge, Tennessee, owned by country music singer Dolly Parton. It features rides, shows, and attractions that celebrate the culture and heritage of the Smoky Mountains.

Attractions

  • Roller Coasters
  • Live Music Shows
  • Cultural Exhibits
  • Craft Demonstrations
